Why modal editing

Less modifier key gymnastics. Better hand/wrist/finger health.

Why vim over emacs + evil?

Vim being a command line program, better integration with other tools. e.g., I can launch vim in WEbstorm's terminal to stage a single line in fugitive.

Why not god-mode, devil-mode?

Emacs lock-in. Works only for emacs. Niche within a niche. While gets rid of modifier key twists, still not composable. Text objects missing. Vim keybindings can cross over to other editors/IDEs. e.g., IdeaVIM, evil, vscode-neovim
